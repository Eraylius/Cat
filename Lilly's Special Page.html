Printing an STL from online websites

Made for Lilly Ramthun

Finding a printable file

  1. Go to your preferred website(Thingiverse, Printables, 3DPrints, etc)
  2. Click on the link or picture of the item you want to print
  3. Click download and wait until download is finished

Extracting downloaded file

  1. Open file explorer
  2. Right click on the downloaded file and click extract
  3. Extract to desired location (where you want the file to be located)

Preparing your file for slicing

  1. Open your slicing software of your choice(EX: Ultimaker Cura)
  2. Plug in your Micro SD card into the USB adapter
  3. Plug your USB adapter into your laptop/desktop
  4. Open your file using the slicer
  5. Determine size, orientation, and positioning
  6. Determine whether the print will need supports
  7. Click slice and wait for slicer to prepare gcode
  8. Click save to removable
  9. Click eject to safely remove micro SD card from laptop/desktop

Preparing your printer

  1. Insert Micro SD card into printer

    • Insert Micro SD card into printer only when powered off. Do not remove Micro SD card when printer is on.

  2. Turn on printer and rehome
  3. Level printing using levelling method of your choice
  4. Select print (on some printers "print from TF card")
  5. Begin print and wait for extruder and bed to get to temp
  6. Adjust your Z-offset for layer height as it begins its initial layers
